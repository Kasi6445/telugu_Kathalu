## Section B — Link Structure

### B3 · onclick on `<div>` or `<button>` used for navigation

| Page | Finding | Status | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| H (homepage) | Category pills: `<button class="nav-pill" onclick="filterCategory('neeti', this)">` — 8 of these, **no href** | ⚠️ | Convert to `<a href="/?cat={name}">` |
| H (homepage) | Cat-grid tiles: `<button class="cat-card cat-bg-neeti" onclick="filterCategory('neeti', null)">` — 8 of these, **no href** | ⚠️ | Convert to `<a href="/?cat={name}">` |
| Story pages | No onclick navigation on divs/buttons — back link is `<a href="/">` | ✅ | — |

**Detail:** Googlebot cannot follow `<button onclick>` to discover category-filtered views. Category pages are not separate URLs (they use `/?cat=neeti` query params handled by JS `popstate`), so the immediate risk is lower than for story links. However, providing `href` attributes converts these to crawlable links and improves accessibility.

### B4 · Story pages reachable within 2–3 hops from homepage via `<a href>`

| Check | Result | Status | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage → story page (1 hop) | **Impossible via static links** — homepage has 0 static story hrefs | ❌ | Pre-render story links at build time |
| Sitemap → story page (1 hop) | ✅ All 62 stories listed in sitemap.xml with clean URLs | ✅ | — |

**Detail:** Googlebot discovers story pages via the sitemap, not via link crawl from the homepage. This means: (a) new pages may not be discovered until the next sitemap crawl, and (b) PageRank does not flow from the homepage to story pages via link equity. The sitemap fallback works for indexation but not for ranking signals.

Manage it via Cloudflare dashboard or accept Cloudflare's managed version as authoritative.

## Bonus Finding — CSS Version Split

The CSS cache-bust version is inconsistent across the 62 story pages:

| CSS version | Pages affected |
|---|---|
| `?v=7` (stale) | **32 pages** |
| `?v=28` (current) | 30 pages |

`lib/seo_writer.py:148` hardcodes `/static/style.css?v=7`. Pages promoted when the version was `?v=28` in `seo_writer.py` (or rebuilt via `backfill_seo.py` when it was temporarily set) got the current version. Any story promoted now will get `?v=7`. The 32 stale pages serve styles from browser cache that may be 21 versions behind.

## Recommended Order of Operations

1. **Apply body-text inlining** (`seo_writer.py` + `scripts/backfill_seo.py`) — biggest indexing lever
2. **Apply homepage static links** (`scripts/build_homepage.py`) — enables link-based discovery
3. **Fix CSS version** (run `node scripts/bump-css-version.js`, fix seo_writer.py constant)
4. **Rebuild all stories** (`python scripts/backfill_seo.py`)
5. **Deploy to Cloudflare Pages** (git push)
6. **Request re-indexing** of 5–10 representative URLs in Google Search Console
7. **Wait 2–3 weeks**, then check Coverage report for thin-content warnings

See `seo-fix-plan.md` for concrete proposed diffs.
